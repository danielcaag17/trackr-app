About Trackr

Trackr is a tool designed to help you analyze and understand human presence in video footage using state-of-the-art detection models. This project was created to provide fast, secure, and private video analysis directly in your browser.

We are committed to improving computer vision accessibility and reducing barriers to entry for researchers and developers.
